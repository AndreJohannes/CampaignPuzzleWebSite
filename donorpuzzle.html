PSYDEH Donor Puzzle

  • The donor puzzle is our way of sharing the the progress of our Crowdfunding campaign with the donor community. We turned an illustrative image from the region of the indigenous people in the Mexican state if Hidalgo into a puzzle with 2500 pieces. Each piece is worth $10usd. For each $10usd donation a new puzzle piece will be added to the image. A donation of $100usd will add 10 pieces. The full image will be visible once we have reached our goal of $25000usd.
    It is our desire to acknowledge each donor. Move the mouse over the image to obtain information on the donor of the corresponding piece.
