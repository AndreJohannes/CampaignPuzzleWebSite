WHY A DONOR PUZZLE?

Here at “Fruits of Change” campaign central, we celebrate you, the global citizen donor, AND progress towards our $25,000 goal – money we will use in these specific ways to build self-reliant communities in Mexico

How do we use the puzzle to highlight campaign progress?
Each of the 2500 puzzle pieces represents a $10usd donation. So, say you donate the equivalent of $100usd, we add 10 more pieces. BOOM!

How does the puzzle celebrate you?
Each puzzle piece is connected to an individual donor! Scroll over the image to find out who donated, how much and from where. One click on the “Global Donors’ Flags” button above reveals a cool visual of global citizens’ strength when united. DOUBLE BOOM!

WHAT IS THE IMAGE?

It’s an embroidered map of Tenango de Doria, Hidalgo, the heart of the Otomí-Tepehua region in which PSYDEH and its native women partners work.

Why an embroidered map?
For centuries, the Region's Otomí people have practiced globally famous hand-embroidery involving figures called “Tenangos".

Why the “Fruits of Change” campaign and Tenangos?
We are more self-relient when we have money in our pocket. PSYDEH’s multi-year program understands this and reflects native women's demand to link community organizing and citizen education with economic empowerment.
Your donation puts money in artisans' pockets in two ways: (1) you invest in an unprecedented Regional Cooperative; and (2) $500 and $1000usd donation levels include an embroidered gift and this money in native women's pockets - YOU win, "Fruits of Change" wins. WOMEN win!
